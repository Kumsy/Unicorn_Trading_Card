Geminate Unicorn

With a benevolent gaze, the unicorns bring eternal luck upon those lucky enough to catch a glimpse...

  • Nature: Tranquil
  • Type: Mythical
  • Average Lifespan: 410,018 years
  • Natural Habitat: Lush Forests
